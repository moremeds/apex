## 5. Risk notes / new findings
- **D6 (NEW) — `domain/strategy → backtest.analysis.dual_macd.*`:** the strategy
  core imports `behavioral_models` and `trade_decision_logger` from the *backtest*
  layer, which the spec says to leave untouched. So `domain/strategy` is **not**
  cleanly separable from backtest as-is. Options: (a) move the two `dual_macd`
  analysis modules out of `backtest.analysis` into `domain/strategy` (or a shared
  `domain` location); (b) treat `domain/strategy` as **not required** for the
  live-signal MVP (signals come from `domain/signals` + rules; strategies/backtest
  are a separate concern) and exclude it from the streaming keep-set. **Recommend
  (b) for the MVP** — the WS/REST signal path needs `domain/signals` + rules, not
  the strategy/playbook framework. Revisit if argon needs strategy-level signals.
